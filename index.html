Welcome to Gourbanbus

With gourbanbus, you won't have to wait too long to find out where the bus is and how long it will take you to arrive. The GPS tracker allows you to quickly monitor and track the bus's whereabouts within the specified region. It facilitates a seamless and trouble-free transfer from one area to another. As these GPS trackers enable real-time tracking of the bus, passengers and bus drivers are able to observe each other's locations in real-time. Therefore, passengers save time and effort. By determining whether or not a passenger is waiting at the bus stop, the driver may plan their route and save time.

Live Passenger Data

The Automated Passenger Counter provides the rider boarding and departure data possible, giving you the information you need to make better decisions for your bus fleet. The Passenger Counters use advanced infrared technology to detect when passengers board and leave your bus.

Driver Information

Support your drivers with an easy-to-use interface where they can sign in, perform inspections, view routes and monitor for passenger information from different stations. By connecting your drivers, you create a moving network of real-time fleet data.

GPS Tracker using QR Code

Help your passengers get the most out of their public transportation experience with the QR code scanner feature that lets them view bus locations, ETAs and routes. Give your passengers access to helpful real-time route and location information from any device with internet access.

Anti-Bunching

Keeping your buses on-schedule and in-sync is vital. Our anti-bunching algorithm accounts for multiple variables (time, distance, season, hour, traffic, etc.) when determining the ideal route progression timing for your bus fleet.
